Frequently asked question

  1. How much is the charges for Card request?

    The charges varies according to the card request you made.

    For card replacement the request fee -> Is Rs.250

    For upgarde Card Type ->

    1.Silver to gold -> Rs.250

    2.Gold to platinum -> Rs.330

  2. My debit card is expired. How I can replace it ?

    You can replace your expired / blocked card from our any branch.

  3. What are the possible reasons for my card not working at ATMs?

    The card might not be working if :

    • The balance is insufficient
    • The daily limit is reached
    • Wrong PIN
    • Damaged card
    • Card is not listed
    • Account is freezed or inoperative
  4. What happens if I enter a wrong PIN or forget my ATM PIN?

    When wrong pin is used you can reenter it or set new card pin

  5. If my card type is Silver can i upgrade it to platinum?

    You can not upgrade directly to platinum you firstly need to upgrade to gold then you upgrade to platinum after specific time period
